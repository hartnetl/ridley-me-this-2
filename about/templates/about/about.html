About

What We Do

Ridley Me This is a non profit organisation looking to helping the conservation of sea turtles. All proceeds are donated to different turtle organisations helping to conserve, protect and breed the 7 different species of sea turtle worldwide With every purchase you make our profits go to these companies. We are a small team and are all passionate about wildlife and conservation. We strive to educate people first and foremost, and welcome any and all questions you may have.

How You Can Help

There are a number of ways you can help, suitable for all ages and lifestyles.

  • Turtle sponsorships make great gifts! For one year that turtle is allocated to you and you can keep up to date with their whereabouts and give it any name of your choosing.
  • Browse our merchandise - these make great gifts for yourself and others. All products are handmade locally at a reduced price for us especially.
  • If you have it to spare, direct donations are so helpful to us and very appreciated. The full amount of this goes towards the turtles.
  • If you're not in a position to spend money, don't you worry we've got you covered! Head over to our learn more page here to read up on ways you can help sea turtles and other wild life without spending a cent
